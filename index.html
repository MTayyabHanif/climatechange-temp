About the Campaign

The Greater Boston Food Bank is a frontline responder in Eastern Massachusetts during this coronavirus crisis.


With more schools and companies closing and residents being asked to self-isolate, the demand for food in our network is increasing.


We are continuing to operate, distributing healthy food via our partner hunger-relief agencies to ensure that our neighbors in need have enough food during this uncertain time.


Your support will help us keep our warehouse open and continue distributing food for our most vulnerable neighbors.


You can help by donating or creating a fundraising team today.
